RS232
Legacy serial ‚Äî CNCs, lab instruments, scales, PLCs

  • Labeled COM, RS232, or Serial

RJ45 (Ethernet)
Ethernet/IP, Modbus TCP, PROFINET, EtherCAT
  • 10 / 100 / 1000 Mbps (Gigabit common)
  • Up to 100 m per cable segment
  • Industrial variants are ruggedized / lockable
  • 8-pin modular jack (larger phone jack)
  • LED indicators for link / activity
  • Labeled ETH, LAN, or Ethernet symbol
M12
Sensors, actuators & Ethernet in harsh environments
  • IP67 / IP68 ‚Äî dust & water proof
  • Vibration-resistant screw lock
  • Standard in robotics & outdoor automation
  • Circular threaded connector (12 mm dia.)
  • 3, 4, 5, 8, or 12 pin options
  • A-code (sensors), D-code (Ethernet), X-code (10G)
USB
Programming, configuration, HMI, data logging
  • USB 2.0: 480 Mbps | USB 3.x: up to 10 Gbps
  • Can supply power (5 V, up to 100 W USB-PD)
  • Cable limit ~5 m without active extension
  • Type-A: rectangular (host side)
  • Type-B: square/beveled (device side)
  • Mini / Micro / USB-C for compact devices
